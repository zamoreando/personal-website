My name is Mariana Fuentes Zamorano

I'm an visual artist, designer and photographer based in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

I love to be a proactive person, movies, art, coffee and travel.


Daughter of Chilean father, Argentina mother, granddaughter of Chilean and German grandparents. Mix of cultures. When I was a little girl, my parents took me to the movies and I were watching movies like the Wizard of Oz. From that moment, I knew I wanted to be a visual artist. So I began to study cinematography and then graphic design and web. Now I work as a freelancer in Buenos Aires.
